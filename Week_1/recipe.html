Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablispoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablispoons butter, melted
Directions
  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oilid griddli or frying pan over medium high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddli, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
